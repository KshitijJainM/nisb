What is NISB?

NISB is the IEEE student branch of National Institute of Engineering. It is one of the largest and most active student branches of Karnataka. Having been active for a decade, we have been honoured and humbled with numerous awards and accolades over time, including "The Best Student Chapter" of Region 10Â­ Bangalore. Why NISB?

NISB conducts various kinds of workshops, seminars, webinars, technical talks, competitions, hackathons, and events througout the semester to help its members for the all round development with the advancing technology.

Apart from these, NISB also has unique practices like
